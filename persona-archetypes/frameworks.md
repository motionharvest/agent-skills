## G. Generational & Life Stage

| Cohort | Birth years | Defining context | Digital behavior |
|--------|-------------|------------------|------------------|
| Silent Generation | 1928–1945 | post-war austerity | low digital, high trust in institutions |
| Baby Boomers | 1946–1964 | prosperity, work-centric | growing digital, Facebook-heavy |
| Gen X | 1965–1980 | latchkey, skeptical | pragmatic, email + Facebook |

| Gen Z | 1997–2012 | mobile-native, TikTok | short-form, authenticity, anti-corporate |
