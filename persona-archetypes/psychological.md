# Psychological Archetype Reference

Deep reference for trait-based and type-based personality frameworks.

---

## Big Five (OCEAN) — Facets

Each trait has 6 facets (NEO-PI-R). Use facets when a persona needs nuance.

### Openness
| Facet | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| Fantasy | imaginative inner life | practical, concrete |
| Aesthetics | values art, beauty | functional over form |
| Feelings | receptive to emotions | reserved emotionally |
| Actions | tries new things | prefers routine |
| Ideas | intellectual curiosity | concrete thinking |
| Values | open to re-examining beliefs | traditional values |

### Conscientiousness
| Facet | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| Competence | capable, effective | self-doubt |
| Order | organized, neat | casual, messy |
| Dutifulness | strict ethics | flexible morals |
| Achievement Striving | ambitious | laid-back |
| Self-Discipline | focused, persistent | procrastinates |
| Deliberation | thinks before acting | impulsive |

### Extraversion
| Facet | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| Warmth | friendly, affectionate | reserved |
| Gregariousness | loves crowds | prefers solitude |
| Assertiveness | dominant, leads | deferential |
| Activity | energetic, fast-paced | leisurely |
| Excitement-Seeking | thrills, stimulation | calm environments |
| Positive Emotions | cheerful, optimistic | subdued |

### Agreeableness
| Facet | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| Trust | assumes good intent | skeptical |
| Straightforwardness | frank, sincere | guarded |
| Altruism | helpful, generous | self-focused |
| Compliance | defers, cooperates | competitive |
| Modesty | humble | self-promoting |
| Tender-Mindedness | sympathetic | tough-minded |

### Neuroticism
| Facet | High | Low |
|-------|------|-----|
| Anxiety | worry-prone | calm under stress |
| Angry Hostility | irritable | even-tempered |
| Depression | prone to sadness | emotionally stable |
| Self-Consciousness | sensitive to judgment | thick-skinned |
| Impulsiveness | difficulty resisting urges | controlled |
| Vulnerability | overwhelmed by stress | resilient |

### Messaging by Big Five Profile

| Profile | Lead with | Avoid |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| High O | novelty, vision, possibilities | "same as always" |
| Low O | proven, reliable, familiar | radical change |
| High C | quality, process, guarantees | chaos, vagueness |
| Low C | easy, flexible, no commitment | complex setup |
| High E | social proof, energy, community | isolation |
| Low E | depth, privacy, self-paced | forced interaction |
| High A | harmony, support, partnership | aggression, competition |
| Low A | ROI, edge, winning | soft sell only |
| High N | reassurance, safety nets | high-stakes without support |
| Low N | bold claims, challenge | over-caution |

## HEXACO — Sixth Dimension

Adds **Honesty-Humility** to Big Five:

| H Factor | High | Low |
|----------|------|-----|
| Sincerity | genuine, unpretentious | manipulative charm |
| Fairness | just, principled | exploitative |
| Greed Avoidance | modest, unmaterialistic | status-seeking |
| Modesty | humble | entitled |

Critical for trust-sensitive products (finance, health, legal).

| Dichotomy | Pole A | Pole B |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| E/I | Extraversion — outer world energy | Introversion — inner world energy |

## DISC — Full Profiles

| Style | Motivated by | Fears | Under stress |
|-------|--------------|-------|--------------|
| **D** Dominance | winning, control | being taken advantage of | becomes dictatorial |
| **I** Influence | recognition, approval | rejection, loss of influence | becomes disorganized |
| **S** Steadiness | cooperation, sincerity | sudden change, loss of security | becomes passive-aggressive |
| **C** Conscientiousness | quality, accuracy | criticism, being wrong | becomes paralyzed |

### DISC → Sales Approach

| Style | Open with | Close with |
|-------|-----------|------------|
| D | bottom line, ROI | direct ask, options |
| I | enthusiasm, vision | social proof, relationship |
| S | reassurance, support | guarantee, step-by-step |
| C | data, evidence | detailed proposal, time to decide |
